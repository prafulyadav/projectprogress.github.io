OUR RELIGION IS HUMANITY

We look forward to the times when empathy and respect will take over the sympathy and pity. As a society, we need to look beyond a disability whether physical or any other. We at Narayan Seva Sansthan, desire for a world for everybody to live in with equal dignity and respect. With a motto to change the kind of perception one looks at a differently able; Narayan Seva Sansthan a not for profit organization originally headquartered in India with over three many decades of perseverance has been serving the noble cause for Physical, Social and Economic Rehabilitation of Differently able and Destitute strata of the society. Set up in 1985 the association furnishes its administrations with more than 480 branches in India and 49 across the globe. An extension to the organization in India, NSS-US chapter was registered in May 2001 acting as a pillar of support since then. We are constantly working to provide better life conditions to the differently able under the mentorship of our president Mr. Prashant Agarwal Ji and the root behind NSS, Padamshri Mr. Kailash ‘Manav’ Ji. An internationally renowned specialized centre vigorously working for disabled and destitute, Narayan Seva Sansthan believes in spreading awareness and extending aid benefits to the needy.
